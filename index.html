How it works

Bus Board allows us Us Station Masters to record and orgaize the details of drivers that are currenntly available, allowing easy and simple organization of bus numbers, destinations, depature and arrival times and bussing fees.

Data is organized in a structural database system making it easily accesible no matter how large data is.

Simple to add, remove, sort and alter the details of drivers that have been registered on the database.
